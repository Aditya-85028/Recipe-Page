This delicious pasta dish combines charred broccoli, chickpeas, and a spicy harissa sauce. It's easy to make and packed with flavor!

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Macros

Calories: 500 kcal

Protein: 15g

Carbohydrates: 60g

Fat: 18g

Ingredients

  • 1 head of broccoli, cut into small florets
  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 2 tbsp harissa paste
  • 300g pasta of your choice
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Spread the broccoli on a baking sheet, drizzle with olive oil, and roast for 10-15 minutes until charred.
  2. In a skillet, heat a tablespoon of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chickpeas and cook until crispy, about 5 minutes.
  3. Cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  4. In a large mixing bowl, combine the roasted broccoli, chickpeas, pasta, and harissa paste. Toss well to coat.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve warm.

Notes

This dish is great served with a sprinkle of fresh herbs, like parsley or basil. Feel free to add extra vegetables or swap out the chickpeas for another protein, like grilled chicken or tofu.
